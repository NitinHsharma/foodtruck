Our Story

Mumbai's Street Food Redefined

Established to bring safe, delicious, and inclusive street food options to city foodies, Snack Shack caters specifically to Vegetarian and Egg-loving palates. We take standard street favorites—buttery rolls, fresh burgers, toasted subs, and hearty rice bowls—and prep them daily using hygienic standards and local spices. Our commitment is simple: no meat, no compromises, just pure flavor.
